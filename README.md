# CredLoop — Student Micro-Economy Platform

CredLoop is a platform where students can exchange skills, rent items, and offer services within a trusted campus ecosystem.

---

## What it does

Students can:

* Exchange skills
* Rent or lend clothes
* Share books and gadgets
* Offer services
* Trade creative work

---

## Core Idea

CredLoop creates a closed-campus marketplace powered by credits instead of only money.

Students earn credits by:

* Teaching skills
* Renting items
* Providing services

Students spend credits on:

* Learning skills
* Renting items
* Borrowing equipment

---

## Problem it solves

* Limited money access for students
* Wastage of unused items
* Lack of skill-sharing platforms
* Low campus collaboration

---

## Why this is different

Unlike platforms like Udemy or OLX, CredLoop is:

* Campus-focused
* Multi-purpose (skills + items + services)
* Credit-based instead of purely money-based
* Built on trust within a college community

---

## How it works

1. Student signs up with college ID
2. Creates a profile with:

   * Skills
   * Items for rent
   * Services offered
3. Earn and spend campus credits through interactions
